UIUC PintoBean Map

The piebald squirl Pinto Bean has become a new unofficial UIUC mascot. However,
Pinto is one of the thousands of squrils here on campus and can be difficult
to find.
This site uses user submissions to plot the locations Pinto Bean has been. .

How it works

the site works using leaflet, which is a JS libraray for inteactive maps.
Leaflet uses Longitude and Latitude to plot points. users can click on the
map to get the points for their submission. Time should also be incuded
in submisison. The map resets every 48 hours
